Live Music Venues

A curated list of venues with live music across Western and Central Massachusetts. This is our research into where jazz and live music happens in the region — not a list of places we've played. If you run a venue and want live jazz, let's talk.

Palmer & Monson Area

Seven Railroads Brewing Co.

1235 Park St, Palmer, MA

Cozy taproom with a dedicated live music corner. The owners are music lovers. Booking: mike@sevenrailroads.beer

sevenrailroads.beer/events

Steaming Tender Restaurant

28 Depot St, Palmer, MA

#1-rated restaurant in Palmer. Historic train station. Patio music in season (they've hosted swing bands). Thu-Sun, upscale dinner crowd. Call (413) 283-2744.

steamingtender.com

Springfield & Surrounds

Theodore's Blues, Booze & BBQ

201 Worthington St, Springfield, MA

Named "Best Blues Club in the Country" (Blues Foundation KBA Award). ~300 capacity. Books nightly. Email mgmt@theodoresbbq.com or call (413) 736-6000.

theodoresbbq.com

Dewey's Jazz Lounge

232 Worthington St, Springfield, MA

Dedicated jazz room opened 2021. Live jazz most weekends, pro sound system. Call (413) 301-8337 or email events@deweyslounge.com.

deweyslounge.com

Nathan Bill's Bar & Restaurant

Springfield, MA

Well-known for live music. Good-sized room, food and drinks, regular local acts.

Pioneer Valley — Northampton, Easthampton, Amherst

Luthier's Co-Op

108 Cottage St, Easthampton, MA

75-cap room, free live music Tue-Sat starting 7pm. Tips + merch (tip bucket provided), 50% off bar tab for band. Book via luthiersbooking@gmail.com (max 3 months out).

luthiers-coop.com

The Parlor Room

32 Masonic St, Northampton, MA

Intimate 50-80 cap nonprofit listening room. BYOB, all ages, great sound. Submit music + video + MA performance history to booking@parlorroom.org.

parlorroom.org

Bishop's Lounge

41 Strong Ave (3rd floor), Northampton, MA

Two floors, two stages. Live bands Fri/Sat, open mics other nights. Operating since 2003. Contact bishopslounge@gmail.com or (413) 586-8900.

bishopslounge.com

13th Floor Music Lounge

Florence, MA

Eclectic venue that books a range of styles. Worth a pitch for a jazz night.

Central Mass — Worcester & Sturbridge

The Blue Room

Worcester, MA

Jazz-focused venue in Worcester. Natural fit for jazz repertoire.

Off The Rails

90 Commercial St, Worcester, MA

Live music + BBQ restaurant. Worth exploring for a jazz dinner set.

offtherailsworcester.com

Cedar Street Grille

Sturbridge, MA

Restaurant with live music. Dinner crowd that could appreciate a jazz combo.

Sturbridge Porterhouse

Sturbridge, MA

Steakhouse with entertainment. Classic supper-club vibe for jazz standards.

Festivals & Events

Springfield Jazz & Roots Festival

Downtown Springfield — July

Free outdoor festival at Stearns Square. Apply to perform — community music is welcomed.

springfieldjazzfest.com

Northampton Jazz Festival

Downtown Northampton — September

Jazz throughout downtown Northampton. Multiple stages, local and regional acts.
