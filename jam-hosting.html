Looking for a Jam Session Home

We're searching for a public venue to host a regular jazz jam session in Western Massachusetts.

What We're Looking For

A brewery, restaurant, bar, or community space willing to host a recurring jazz jam session. We bring the musicians, the equipment, and the energy. You provide the space and the audience.

Format
Open jam session — our trio provides the house band, guest musicians sit in
Frequency
Weekly or bi-weekly, depending on venue preference
Duration
2-3 hours, typically evening
Area
Palmer, Monson, Springfield, or surrounding towns

Why Host a Jam Session?

A regular jam night brings a unique crowd — musicians, music lovers, and curious locals who want something different from the usual bar scene.

  • Consistent weekly/bi-weekly event — Something your regulars can count on and look forward to
  • Musician community — Jazz players travel to find good jams, bringing new faces (and tabs) to your venue
  • Low cost, high reward — We're not asking for guarantees; we're looking for a partnership
  • Unique atmosphere — Live jazz improvisation creates energy that recorded music can't match
  • Local press potential — "New jazz jam night at [your venue]" is a story worth telling

What We Bring

GTD Trio serves as the house band — guitar, tuba (providing bass lines), and drums. We handle the musical logistics: calling tunes, managing sit-ins, keeping the session moving. We can bring our own small PA if needed, or work with whatever sound setup you have.

We're flexible on compensation structure — door split, tip jar, food/drink trade, or some combination. The goal is to build something sustainable that works for everyone.

Let's Talk

If you run a venue and this sounds interesting, we'd love to chat. No commitment — just a conversation about whether this could work for your space.
