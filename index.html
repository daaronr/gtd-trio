The Band

We're a jazz combo rooted in the Great American Songbook, with a deep love for the music of the late 1940s through the 1960s — and a willingness to go anywhere the music takes us, from old-timey swing to modern post-bop and beyond.

Our sound is built on a distinctive foundation: tuba providing the bass lines — walking bass, swing, Latin grooves, funk — alongside drums and guitar. Add in trombone and flugelhorn for front-line flexibility, and you get a combo that's compact enough for a corner of a restaurant and full enough to fill a club.

We play standards, songbook classics, some original compositions and arrangements. Whether it's a dinner set, a cocktail hour, a festival stage, or a late-night session, we bring the real thing: improvisation, interaction, and music that makes people lean in and listen.
